Timeline

2003

Android Inc. was founded by Andy Rubin, Rich Miner, Nick Sears, and Chris White.

2005

Google acquired Android Inc. and began developing the Android platform.

2008

The first Android device, the HTC Dream (also known as the T-Mobile G1), was released.
